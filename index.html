Places App Demo

iPhone and iPad App, was on the App Store

This app is essentially a contacts app, except that each contact is placed on a map. The app allows to customize the pins displayed on the map, add any fields to the contacts and then filter them, etc. When zooming out, the pins are collapsed and a number is displayed.
